Tables Page

HTML features tables with simple implementaion and wide customization:

Structure:

Table are made using <table> and <tr> for rows, <td> for columns and <th> for headers.

It has a few options like border inside <table> to make a frame around it and <bgcolor> for coloring the fields

It has options for header & footers to distingush it from table body and caption to describe a field

It supports merging cells using <rowspan> and <colspan> which will be illusterted in the syntax and example

Syntax:

<table border="1">

        <caption>
Ranking of teams in the Champions League
        </caption>

        <thead bgcolor="#7c7775">
            <tr>
                <th>Points</th>
                <th>Game</th>
                <th>Teams</th>
                <th>Arrangement</th>
            </tr>
        </thead>

        <tbody bgcolor="#b9b1ae">
            <tr>
                <td>18</td>
                <td>6</td>
                <td>Liverpool</td>
                <td>1</td>
            </tr>
            <tr>
                <td>15</td>
                <td>6</td>
                <td>Barcelona</td>
                <td>2</td>
            </tr>
    
        </tbody>

        <tfoot bgcolor="#b9b1ae">
            <td  align="right" colspan="4">Total</td>
        </tfoot>
    </table>

Example:

Ranking of teams in the Champions League
Points Game Teams Arrangement
18 6 Liverpool 1
15 6 Barcelona 2
Total
